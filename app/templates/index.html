Sudoku

Rules

  • You must fill each empty box with a number from 1 to 9.
  • Each row, column, and 3x3 subgrid must contain all numbers 1-9.
  • Each row, column, and 3x3 subgrid cannot have repeat numbers.
  • Click the 'Verify' button to check your answers.
  • Click the 'Solve' button to reveal the correct puzzle.
